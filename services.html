Got Questions?

Frequently Asked Questions

Can't find your answer here? Reach out to us directly — we're happy to help.

Contact Us →
How far in advance should I book? +

We recommend booking at least 2–4 weeks in advance for smaller events, and 6–8 weeks for larger events (150+ guests). That said, we do accommodate last-minute requests where possible — just DM us and we'll do our best!

Do you cater outside of Brooklyn? +

Yes! While we're based in Brooklyn, we serve clients across all five boroughs of New York City and the surrounding New Jersey and Connecticut areas. Travel fees may apply for events outside Brooklyn. Contact us to confirm your location.

Can you accommodate dietary restrictions? +

Absolutely. We offer halal options, vegetarian-friendly items, and can work around most common allergies. Please let us know your requirements when you get in touch so we can build a menu that works for all your guests.

What is your minimum order / guest count? +

We cater to events of all sizes — from intimate gatherings of 10 to large celebrations of 500+. For drop-off orders, a minimum order value applies. Get in touch and we'll give you a clear quote based on your specific needs.

Do you provide service staff? +

Yes — our Signature and Luxe packages include a dedicated service team for set-up, serving, and breakdown. For our Essentials package, drop-off delivery is available. Our team always arrives professionally dressed and ready to represent your event beautifully.

How do I secure my booking? +

Once we've agreed on your menu and service details, a deposit is required to confirm your date. The remaining balance is due 48–72 hours before your event. We accept multiple payment methods — ask us for details.
