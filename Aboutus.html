How It All Began

Brooklyn's Finest Bites & Sips

Mocktail Canapés was born from a simple but powerful belief: that every event — no matter the size — deserves food that is as beautiful to look at as it is delicious to eat.

We started with a passion for crafting finger foods, canapés, and bespoke sips that don't just nourish but tell a story. What began as a labour of love in Brooklyn has grown into a full-service luxury catering experience trusted by families, corporations, and event planners across New York.

Every tray we style, every mocktail we craft, and every small chop we prepare carries the same intention — to make your guests pause, savour, and remember.

What Drives Us

Our Core Values

✨

Luxury in Every Detail

From the garnish on a mocktail to the fold of a serviette, we believe luxury lives in the small things. Nothing leaves our kitchen without intention.

🍽️

Artistry on the Plate

Food is our canvas. Every platter, tray, and cup is styled to delight the eye before it pleases the palate — because presentation is part of the experience.

🤝

People First

We listen before we cook. Every event is different, every host has a vision, and our job is to bring that vision to life with warmth, care, and professionalism.

🌿

Quality Ingredients

We source thoughtfully and cook with intention. Only the freshest ingredients make it into our recipes — because great food starts before it hits the heat.

🎉

Joy in Every Bite

We don't just cater events — we create memories. The laughter, the "oh wow" moments, the second helpings — that's what we're truly here for.

🏙️

Proudly Brooklyn

Brooklyn is in our DNA. Its energy, its diversity, its creative spirit — all of it lives in our food and the way we show up for our community.

Meet the Founder

The Heart Behind the Brand

Mocktail Canapés was founded with a clear mission — to bring restaurant-quality presentation and taste to every private event, party, and corporate gathering in Brooklyn and beyond.

With a background deeply rooted in hospitality and a genuine love for food artistry, our founder built this brand from the ground up with one non-negotiable standard: every client deserves a luxurious experience, no matter the budget or scale.

"Food is how we show love. When someone trusts us with their event, they 're trusting us with their memories — and that responsibility is something we never take lightly."
